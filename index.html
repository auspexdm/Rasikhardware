About Us

Rasik Hardware,
Alibag

At Rasik Hardware, we are more than just a hardware store — we are your trusted partner in building, repairing, and enhancing spaces. Established in Alibag, we have proudly served homeowners, contractors, and businesses for years with quality products, expert guidance, and reliable service.

Our store offers a comprehensive range of hardware, tools, paints, plumbing supplies, electrical fittings, construction materials, and home improvement essentials — all sourced from trusted brands to ensure durability and performance.

We believe in combining quality, affordability, and customer satisfaction, making sure every customer finds exactly what they need, whether it's for a small DIY project or a large-scale construction job.
